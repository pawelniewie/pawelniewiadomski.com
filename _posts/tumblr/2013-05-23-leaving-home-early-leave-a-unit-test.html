I sometimes need to finish when I’m half way done. I usually left a note in the code for me (or on the desk), but what I find works best is writing a simple unit test that will remind you tomorrow.

You are already writing your tests for the code, right? So why not to add a reminder there.  It’s five minutes work :-)
